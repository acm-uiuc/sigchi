SIGCHI

We are SIGCHI, ACM's special interest group for Human-Computer Interaction. CHI is a multidisciplinary field within computer science that focuses on the interaction between humans and computers. We look at how technology can be designed and engineered to help humans. Check out our Coda.io doc to stay updated this semester!
